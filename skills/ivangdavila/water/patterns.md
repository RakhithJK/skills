## Common Hydration Patterns

### Meal-Based
- Water with every meal (most common)
- Only lunch/dinner, skip breakfast
- Tea/coffee morning, water rest of day

### Activity-Based
- Extra after exercise/gym
- More on hot days
- Water after coffee (self-compensating)

### Work Patterns
- Desk bottle sipping throughout day
- Only drinks at breaks
- Forgets until thirsty

## Pattern Detection

Watch for:
- Consistent mentions at same times → schedule
- Activity triggers → correlations
- Gaps or complaints → flags

## Baseline Reference

General adult needs: ~2-3L daily (includes food water content)
Don't cite numbers to user. Track pattern, not targets.

High-need situations (note silently):
- Hot weather
- Exercise
- Illness/fever
- Alcohol consumption
- High caffeine intake
